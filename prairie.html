Daylight Prairie

Ring the bell towers and reveal your path

Daylight Prairie, often shortened to Prairie, is a natural prairie field once home to a lost farming civilization. It is the second Realm the player visits. Differing from the unique Realm layout of Isle of Dawn, Daylight Prairie is separated into eight areas[1] with loading screens between them all. While the game does not explicitly mention the names of all the areas, they can be derived from landmarks and developer mentions. Daylight Prairie holds 8 Regular Spirits and 14 Seasonal Spirits. Locations of all available Children of Light can be found on the Children of Light page.
